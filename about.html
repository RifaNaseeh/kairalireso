Learn About Us

            Kairali Reso was launched in the year 1994 at Kanjirappalli. It was founded by Mr. Ajas who was dedicated to beginning his own business since his younger age. His determination has helped him grow his business. Currently, Mr. MA Hadi joined as a working partner. Their dedication and hard work have bought them clients in every district of Kerala. Now being experts in the field for 28 years, the team engages with management facilities in Apartments, Colleges, Hospitals, Schools, Hotels and shopping malls. All cleaning materials used during the process are self-manufactured and customized to meet the needs of the cleaning environments which makes their projects done neat, effective and their clients contented. The specialty of their products also includes being eco-friendly and favourable to the sewage.

          The visible success of their venture has also encouraged them to begin complementary works like recruiting staff of any designation to hospitals and providing efficient security services, which are also gaining pace energetically.
